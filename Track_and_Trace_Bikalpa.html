IFC Model: material roles and dimensions
IFC export · 6 beams: 2 vertical columns (2 faces) + 4 roof beams under tarp · ring & ropes · flooring + brick sokkel
↯
From the full ETH inventory we selected 7 material types; everything else stays in stock. The IFC model shows 8 big trusses: 4 laid flat as the floor structure + 4 on walls. 6 beams (19×9 cm) in two roles: 2 vertical columns on 2 opposite open faces (cut to 270 cm), and 4 roof beams acting as hip rafters from the central ring to the 4 corners (350 cm). Floor deck from flooring boards (11.5 cm wide × 1.5 cm thick) on a brick sokkel that levels the trusses off the ground. A central ring (old steering wheel) with ropes ties the 4 roof beams together, and a single tarpaulin draped over them completes the roof. The tarp is the only new purchase in the whole design, and it comes off cleanly at the end.
